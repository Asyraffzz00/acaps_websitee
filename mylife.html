My name is Muhammad Asyraf bin Syamsu Akmal, and I am 21 years old in 2025. I live in a peaceful village located in the Jelebu district of Negeri Sembilan. I was born in HTAN in Kuala Pilah, Negeri Sembilan. In the photo, you'll see my family: to my right is my mother, Rosilawati binti Md. Akib (48), and beside her is my sister, Nurul Imanina binti Syamsu Akmal (23). Next to her is my beloved grandmother, Fauziah binti Ariffin (69), followed by my aunt, Lily Harianty binti Md. Akib (38). Beside her is my other aunt, Siti Faraziah binti Md. Akib (35), and on the far left is my uncle, Md Rasyidi bin Md. Akib (33). I grew up in this small but close-knit family after my parents, who originally came from Indonesia, separated in 2015. Despite the challenges, we’ve always been there for each other, and this bond continues to hold us together.
